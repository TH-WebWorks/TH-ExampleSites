Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We typically respond to emergency calls within 1-2 hours. For urgent situations, we prioritize your call and dispatch our nearest available technician.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all projects. Our estimates include detailed pricing and project timelines.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. We also offer financing options for larger projects.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, all our work comes with a warranty. The specific warranty terms depend on the type of service and materials used.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our technicians are licensed, insured, and undergo regular training to maintain the highest standards of service.

Do you service commercial properties?

Yes, we provide services for both residential and commercial properties, including office buildings, retail spaces, and industrial facilities.
