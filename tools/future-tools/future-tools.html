Future Tools

Here are some ideas for powerful features we may add to the T&H Internal Toolkit. Have a suggestion? Let us know!

  • Proposal Generator

    Auto-generate branded proposals or quotes from client intake data. Export as PDF or send directly to clients.

  • Client Portal

    A secure area for clients to view project status, invoices, and upload/download files.

  • Content Calendar

    A shared calendar for scheduling blog posts, social media, and project milestones.

  • Asset Library

    Centralized storage for logos, brand assets, stock images, and reusable code snippets.

  • Internal Knowledge Base

    Quick access to SOPs, onboarding docs, troubleshooting guides, and best practices.

  • Time Tracking & Invoicing

    Simple time tracking for projects and automated invoice generation.

  • Feedback & QA Tool

    Collect client feedback on live sites or designs, with screenshot and annotation support.

  • Automated Reporting

    Generate website analytics or SEO reports for clients with one click.

  • Team Chat or Announcements

    Internal communication or announcement board for the team.

  • Integrations

    Quick links or integrations with tools you already use (Trello, Slack, Figma, etc.).
